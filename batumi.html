About Batumi

Batumi is a vibrant seaside city located on the Black Sea coast of Georgia. It is known for its modern architecture, beautiful beaches, and bustling atmosphere.

Explore Batumi's iconic landmarks, such as the Alphabetic Tower, Batumi Boulevard, and the Batumi Botanical Garden. Relax on the city's sandy beaches, enjoy a leisurely stroll along the seaside promenade, and take in breathtaking views of the Black Sea.

Experience Batumi's vibrant nightlife by visiting its numerous bars, clubs, and restaurants. Don't miss the opportunity to sample delicious Georgian cuisine, including fresh seafood dishes and local specialties like adjarian khachapuri.

architectur and Landmarks

Batumi is renowned for its innovative architecture, with a skyline punctuated by futuristic skyscrapers, avant-garde structures, and iconic landmarks. The Batumi Boulevard, featuring the Alphabet Tower and the Ferris wheel, offers panoramic views of the city and the sea. The Batumi Sea Port, with its distinctive lighthouse and marina, serves as a focal point for maritime activities and leisure pursuits. Additionally, the Batumi Botanical Garden, located just outside the city, showcases a diverse collection of flora from around the world and provides a tranquil escape amidst lush greenery and scenic vistas.
